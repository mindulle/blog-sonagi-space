# EAP vs PEAP

EAP (Extensible Authentication Protocol) is an authentication framework providing a general method for transport and authentication, supporting various authentication methods. PEAP (Protected EAP) is an EAP protocol that encapsulates EAP within an encrypted and authenticated TLS tunnel. This protect
s the EAP authentication process, making it more secure than standard EAP.
