# Descriptive Analytics

Descriptive Analytics is one of the fundamental types of Data Analytics that provides insight into the past. As a Data Analyst, utilizing Descriptive Analytics involves the technique of using historical data to understand changes that have occurred in a business over time. Primarily concerned with t
he “what has happened” aspect, it analyzes raw data from the past to draw inferences and identify patterns and trends. This helps companies understand their strengths, weaknesses and pinpoint operatio
nal problems, setting the stage for accurate Business Intelligence and decision-making processes.
