# Reconnaissance

Reconnaissance is the first phase of a cyber-attack, during which attackers gather as much information as possible about a target system, network, or organization. The goal of reconnaissance is to identify potential vulnerabilities, entry points, and other valuable details that can be exploited in s
ubsequent attack phases.
