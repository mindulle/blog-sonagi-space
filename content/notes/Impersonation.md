# Impersonation

Impersonation in cybersecurity refers to an attack technique where a threat actor pretends to be a legitimate person or entity to deceive individuals, systems, or organizations. This tactic is commonly used in social engineering attacks to gain unauthorized access to sensitive information, resources
, or systems.
