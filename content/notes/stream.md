# Stream

Cloudflare Stream is a video streaming platform that makes it easy to upload, encode, and deliver videos globally. It handles all the complexities of video streaming, including encoding for different devices and network conditions, providing a reliable and high-quality viewing experience. Stream int
egrates seamlessly with Cloudflare's CDN, ensuring fast and efficient video delivery to users around the world.

Visit the following resources to learn more:

- [@official@Cloudflare Stream - Cloudflare Documentation](https://www.cloudflare.com/developer-platform/products/cloudflare-stream/)
- [@official@Cloudflare Stream - Cloudflare Blog](https://blog.cloudflare.com/tag/cloudflare-stream/)
