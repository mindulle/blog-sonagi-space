# Sales Performance

Sales performance refers to how well a company or individual is doing in terms of generating revenue through sales activities. It involves tracking, analyzing, and improving various metrics like sales volume, revenue growth, conversion rates, and customer acquisition cost to understand what's workin
g and what's not in the sales process. This understanding helps businesses make informed decisions to optimize their sales strategies and achieve their revenue targets.
