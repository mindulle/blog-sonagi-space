# Avalanche

Avalanche is a high-throughput, open-source platform for launching decentralized finance (DeFi) applications and enterprise blockchain deployments in one interoperable, highly scalable ecosystem. It distinguishes itself through its unique consensus mechanism, which allows for faster transaction fina
lity compared to many other blockchain networks. It also supports the creation of custom blockchains and digital assets, offering developers flexibility and control over their applications.
