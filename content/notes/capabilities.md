# Capabilities under Vision & Mission

The role of a Product Manager is multifaceted and one important aspect of that role revolves around setting and understanding the vision and mission. A vision is a long-term goal or aspiration for a product and encompasses the overall direction or strategy for the product. Conversely, a mission stat
ement focuses more on the present, describing the purpose of the product and who it serves. These aspects help drive the decision-making process in product management. Product Managers utilize these t
ools to align the product team and the company as a whole. They make strategic decisions and formulate plans based on the foundation set by the vision and mission. By leveraging these capabilities, Product Managers are able to make informed decisions that propel the product towards success.
