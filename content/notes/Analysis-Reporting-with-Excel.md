# Excel

Excel is a powerful tool utilized by data analysts worldwide to store, manipulate, and analyze data. It offers a vast array of features such as pivot tables, graphs and a powerful suite of formulas and functions to help sift through large sets of data. A data analyst uses Excel to perform a wide ran
ge of tasks, from simple data entry and cleaning, to more complex statistical analysis and predictive modeling. Proficiency in Excel is often a key requirement for a data analyst, as its versatility a
nd ubiquity make it an indispensable tool in the field of data analysis.
