# Box

Box is a popular cloud storage service that provides individuals and businesses with a platform to securely store, share, and access files and documents from any device. Box is known for its emphasis on security and collaboration features, making it an ideal choice for businesses who want a secure w
ay to share and collaborate on files with their teams.
