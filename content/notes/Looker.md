# Looker

Looker is a Google cloud-based business intelligence and data analytics platform. It allows users to explore, analyze, and visualize data to gain insights and make data-driven decisions. Looker is known for its ability to connect to various data sources, create custom dashboards, and generate report
s. It also facilitates the integration of analytics, visualizations, and relevant information into business processes.
