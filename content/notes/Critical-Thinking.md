# Critical Thinking

Critical thinking involves analyzing information objectively and forming a reasoned judgment. It's about evaluating evidence, identifying assumptions, and considering different perspectives to arrive at well-supported conclusions. This process helps in problem-solving and decision-making by ensuring
 that conclusions are logical and based on facts rather than emotions or biases.
