# Avoid Direct Payments

Avoiding direct payments is a crucial aspect of UX design that can lead to favorable conscious evaluations from users. Direct payments refer to instances where users are required to pay for your product or service upfront, which can create a negative perception and less willingness to engage. By fin
ding alternative ways to monetize or offer premium features, you can create an enjoyable experience and encourage users to appreciate and invest in your offerings without feeling forced.
