# Reverse ETL

Reverse ETL is the process of extracting data from a data warehouse, transforming it to fit the requirements of operational systems, and then loading it into those other systems. This approach contrasts with traditional ETL, where data is extracted from operational systems, transformed, and loaded i
nto a data warehouse.
