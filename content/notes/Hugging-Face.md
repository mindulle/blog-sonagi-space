# Hugging Face

Hugging Face is a leading AI company and open-source platform that provides tools, models, and libraries for natural language processing (NLP), computer vision, and other machine learning tasks. It is best known for its "Transformers" library, which simplifies the use of pre-trained models like BERT
, GPT, T5, and CLIP, making them accessible for tasks such as text classification, translation, summarization, and image recognition.
