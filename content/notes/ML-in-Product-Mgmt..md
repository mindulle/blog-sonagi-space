# ML in Product Management 

Machine Learning (ML) is revolutionizing various industries and the field of product management is no exception. In a dynamic digital era, product managers are leveraging machine learning techniques to drive product innovation, better understand customer behavior, and forecast trends. With ML, data 
can be processed at an immense speed allowing product managers to make data-driven decisions and anticipate the future needs of the market, thus creating products that resonate with target audiences. 
Its contribution to predictive and behavioral analytics, customer segmentation and pricing strategy makes ML an essential tool for modern-day Product Management.
