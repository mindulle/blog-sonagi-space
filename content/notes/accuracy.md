# Accuracy

Accuracy refers to the degree to which data correctly reflects the real-world object or event it is intended to represent. It ensures that the data values are correct, reliable, and free from errors, misrepresentations, or inconsistencies. High accuracy is crucial for making sound business decisions
 and generating trustworthy insights.
