# Key Exchange

Key exchange refers to the processes and protocols used to securely share cryptographic keys between parties. This allows them to then use those keys for encrypting and decrypting messages, ensuring confidentiality and integrity of their communication. Without a secure method for sharing keys, the s
trength of any encryption algorithm is compromised, as an attacker could simply intercept the key and decrypt the messages.
