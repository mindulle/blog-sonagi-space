# Dynamic Forms

Dynamic forms in Angular are a flexible way to create forms where the structure (such as form fields and validation rules) is generated at runtime, rather than being hardcoded. By using Angular's `FormBuilder` and `FormGroup`, you can dynamically add, remove, or modify form controls based on user in
put, data fetched from a server, or other logic. This approach allows for creating complex forms that can adapt to different user scenarios, reducing the need for multiple form templates and making th
e codebase more maintainable and scalable.

Learn more from the following resources:

- [@official@Dynamic Forms Documentation](https://angular.dev/guide/forms/dynamic-forms)
