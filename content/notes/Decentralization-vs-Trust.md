# Decentralization vs. Trust

Decentralization refers to the distribution of power and control away from a central authority. Trust, in this context, is the reliance on a third party or system to act honestly and reliably. The relationship between the two lies in how decentralization aims to reduce or eliminate the need for trus
t in a single entity by distributing it across a network.
