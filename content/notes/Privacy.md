# Privacy

Privacy refers to the right of individuals to control how their personal information is collected, used, and shared. It encompasses various aspects, including data security, confidentiality, and compliance with regulations like GDPR and CCPA. Protecting privacy involves implementing policies and pro
cedures to safeguard sensitive data and ensuring transparency in data handling practices.
