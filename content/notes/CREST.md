# CREST

CREST is a non-profit, accreditation and certification body that represents the technical information security industry. Established in 2008, its mission is to promote the development and professionalization of the cyber security sector. CREST provides certifications for individuals and accreditatio
n for companies, helping customers find knowledgeable and experienced professionals in the field.
