# CompTIA Network+

The CompTIA Network+ is a highly sought-after certification for IT professionals who aim to build a solid foundation in networking concepts and practices. This certification is vendor-neutral, meaning that it covers a broad range of knowledge that can be applied to various network technologies, prod
ucts, and solutions. The Network+ certification is designed for beginners in the world of IT networking, and it is recommended that you first obtain the CompTIA A+ certification before moving on to Ne
twork+.
