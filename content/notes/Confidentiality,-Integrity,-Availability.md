# Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability

The CIA Triad is directly applicable in AI Red Teaming. Confidentiality tests focus on preventing leakage of training data or proprietary model details. Integrity tests probe for susceptibility to data poisoning or model manipulation. Availability tests assess resilience against denial-of-service at
tacks targeting the AI model or its supporting infrastructure.
