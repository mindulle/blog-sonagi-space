# Preparation

The **Preparation** phase in incident response involves establishing and maintaining the tools, policies, and procedures necessary to handle security incidents effectively. This includes creating an incident response plan, defining roles and responsibilities, training staff, and ensuring that approp
riate technologies, such as monitoring systems and logging tools, are in place to detect and respond to incidents. Preparation also involves conducting regular drills, threat intelligence gathering, a
nd vulnerability assessments to enhance readiness, ensuring the organization is equipped to mitigate potential security threats quickly and efficiently.
