# Stay Consistent

Consistency is vital because it ensures that your code integrates seamlessly with the rest of the project and maintains a coherent structure that is easy to understand and maintain. It also ensures that the system remains stable and reliable over time as it evolves. To achieve consistency, we recomm
end the following tips: familiarize yourself with the project's design patterns and conventions, consider the impact of changes on the entire system, work closely with other team members to ensure ali
gnment, and always prioritize consistency and maintainability over temporary optimizations or shortcuts. Now, let's delve deeper into these tips to help you become a better code reviewer.
