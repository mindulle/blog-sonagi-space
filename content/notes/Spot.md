# Spotlights

Spotlights are a common type of light in computer graphics and games that mimic the behavior of real-world spotlights. They offer a range of parameters to adjust their behavior, such as radius, cone angle, falloff, and intensity Spotlights are readily available out of the box in both Unreal and Unit
y game engines, making them an accessible and powerful tool for adding realistic and dynamic lighting to your scenes.
