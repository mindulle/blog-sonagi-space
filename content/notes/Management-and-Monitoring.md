# Management and Monitoring

Management and monitoring patterns focus on keeping cloud applications observable, configurable, and operable at scale. They include patterns such as externalized configuration, health endpoint monitoring, and the Ambassador pattern, helping teams gain visibility into running systems and manage them
 effectively without requiring application restarts or redeployments.
