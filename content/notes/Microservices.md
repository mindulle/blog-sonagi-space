# Microservices

Microservices architecture structures applications as loosely coupled, independently deployable services focused on specific business capabilities. Communicate via HTTP or messaging. Enables scalability, flexibility, diverse technologies, and continuous deployment but adds complexity in communicatio
n and orchestration.
