# Top-P Sampling

Top-P sampling, also known as nucleus sampling, is a technique used in language models to generate text. Instead of considering all possible next words, it focuses on the smallest set of words whose cumulative probability exceeds a threshold 'P'. Unlike Top-K's fixed number, Top-P dynamically adjust
s based on the probability distribution. Low values (0.1-0.5) produce focused outputs, medium (0.6-0.9) balance creativity and coherence, and high (0.9-0.99) enable creative diversity.
