# Pivot / Unpivot Operations

Pivot and unpivot operations transform the structure of data within a table. Pivoting rotates rows into columns, aggregating data based on common values. Conversely, unpivoting transforms columns into rows, often expanding a table's length while reducing its width. These operations are useful for re
shaping data for reporting or analysis.
