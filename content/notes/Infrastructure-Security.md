# Infrastructure Security

AI Red Teamers assess the security posture of the infrastructure hosting AI models (cloud environments, servers, containers). They look for misconfigurations, unpatched systems, insecure network setups, or inadequate access controls that could allow compromise of the AI system or leakage of sensitiv
e data/models.
