# Service Design 

Service Design refers to the process of planning and organizing a business's resources (people, infrastructure, materials, etc.) to directly improve the service's quality, interactions between service provider and clients, and the customer's experience. For a Product Manager, it's a crucial practice
 as it gives them a broader understanding of their product's lifecycle and interactions with the end users. This process aids in crafting or refining products to ensure alignment with customer needs a
nd provide superior user experience.
