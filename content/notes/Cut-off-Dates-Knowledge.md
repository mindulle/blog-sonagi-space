# Sampling Parameters

Sampling parameters are settings that control the randomness and creativity of the text generated by the LLM. They influence how the model chooses the next word in a sequence, impacting the overall coherence, diversity, and relevance of the output. Adjusting these parameters allows users to fine-tun
e the LLM's responses for specific applications, balancing between predictable, safe outputs and more imaginative, exploratory ones.
