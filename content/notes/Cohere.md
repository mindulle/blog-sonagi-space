# Cohere

Cohere offers a suite of closed large language models (LLMs) designed for various natural language processing tasks. These models are accessible through an API and are trained and maintained by Cohere, meaning users don't have to worry about the complexities of training and fine-tuning their own mod
els from scratch. Cohere focuses on providing enterprise-grade NLP solutions emphasizing safety, reliability, and ease of integration.
