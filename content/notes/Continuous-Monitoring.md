# Continuous Monitoring

AI Red Teamers assess the effectiveness of continuous monitoring systems by attempting attacks and observing if detection mechanisms trigger appropriate alerts and responses. They test if monitoring covers AI-specific anomalies (like sudden shifts in output toxicity or unexpected resource consumptio
n by the model) in addition to standard infrastructure monitoring.
