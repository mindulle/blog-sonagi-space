# CEH

**Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)** is an advanced certification focused on equipping cybersecurity professionals with the knowledge and skills required to defend against the continuously evolving landscape of cyber threats. This certification is facilitated by the EC-Council, an internationally reco
gnized organization for information security certifications.

Visit the following resources to learn more:

- [@official@CEH](https://www.eccouncil.org/train-certify/certified-ethical-hacker-ceh/)
- [@article@Certified Ethical Hacker Online Training](https://iclass.eccouncil.org/our-courses/certified-ethical-hacker-ceh/)
