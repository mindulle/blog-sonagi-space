# Building Your Portfolio

A portfolio showcases your BI skills and experience to potential employers. It's a collection of projects, visualizations, and analyses that demonstrate your ability to solve business problems using data. A strong portfolio highlights your technical proficiency, analytical thinking, and communicatio
n skills, giving tangible evidence of your capabilities beyond a resume.
