# Threat Intelligence and Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) Fundamentals

Threat intelligence involves gathering and analyzing information about potential threats and adversaries. OSINT, or Open-Source Intelligence, is a specific type of threat intelligence that focuses on collecting information from publicly available sources, such as news articles, social media, and pub
lic records. By combining and analyzing this data, security professionals can gain insights into attacker motivations, tactics, and infrastructure, enabling them to proactively defend against cyberatt
acks.
