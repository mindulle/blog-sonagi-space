# Cryptowallets

Cryptowallets are tools that allow users to manage, store, and transact with cryptocurrencies. They don't actually hold the cryptocurrency itself; instead, they store the private keys needed to access and control your digital assets on the blockchain. These wallets come in various forms, including s
oftware, hardware, and paper, each offering different levels of security and convenience.
