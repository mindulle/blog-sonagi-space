# iotop

`iotop` is an essential command-line utility that provides real-time insights into the input/output (I/O) activities of processes running on your system. This tool is particularly useful when monitoring and managing your PostgreSQL database's performance, as it helps system administrators or databas
e developers to identify processes with high I/O, leading to potential bottlenecks or server optimization opportunities.
