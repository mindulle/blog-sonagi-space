# Business Model Inspirator

A Business Model Inspirator is a tool or method that helps you to generate new or creative ideas for the strategic, operational, and financial aspects of a business. It helps entrepreneurs, startups, and established companies to explore different ways of designing or improving their business models 
by drawing inspiration from various sources.
