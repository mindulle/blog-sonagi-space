# Database

A database is an organized, structured collection of electronic data that is stored, managed, and accessed via a computer system, usually controlled by a Database Management System (DBMS). Databases organize various types of data, such as words, numbers, images, and videos, allowing users to easily 
retrieve, update, and modify it for various purposes, from managing customer information to analyzing business processes.
