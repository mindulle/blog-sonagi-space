# Predictive analysis

Predictive analysis is a crucial type of data analytics that any competent data analyst should comprehend. It refers to the practice of extracting information from existing data sets in order to determine patterns and forecast future outcomes and trends. Data analysts apply statistical algorithms, m
achine learning techniques, and artificial intelligence to the data to anticipate future results. Predictive analysis enables organizations to be proactive, forward-thinking, and strategic by providin
g them valuable insights on future occurrences. It's a powerful tool that gives companies a significant competitive edge by enabling risk management, opportunity identification, and strategic decision-making.
