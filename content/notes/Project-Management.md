# Project Management

Project management involves planning, organizing, and overseeing the completion of specific goals. It encompasses defining project objectives, creating timelines, allocating resources, and managing risks to ensure projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to the required quality. Effective 
project management relies on clear communication, collaboration, and problem-solving skills to navigate challenges and keep stakeholders aligned.
