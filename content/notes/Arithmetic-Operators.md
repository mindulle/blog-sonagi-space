# Arithmetic Operators

Arithmetic operators are fundamental building blocks in C++ programming, allowing you to perform mathematical calculations directly within your code. These operators work on numeric data types such as integers and floating-point numbers, enabling addition (+), subtraction (-), multiplication (\*), d
ivision (/), and modulus (%) operations. Furthermore, increment (++) and decrement (--) operators provide concise ways to increase or decrease variable values by one.
