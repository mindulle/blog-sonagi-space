# Insurance dApps

Insurance dApps can streamline and automate various insurance processes. For example, claims processing can be automated using smart contracts that trigger payouts when specific conditions are met, verified by oracles providing external data. This reduces fraud, speeds up payouts, and lowers adminis
trative costs. Policy management can also be improved by storing policy details on the blockchain, ensuring transparency and immutability. Furthermore, new insurance models like peer-to-peer insurance
 can be facilitated, where users pool resources and share risks directly, cutting out traditional intermediaries.
