# Virtual Private Networks (VPNs)

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) creates a secure, encrypted connection over a less secure network, like the internet. It essentially extends a private network across a public one, allowing users to send and receive data as if their devices were directly connected to the private network. This is achi
eved by routing the user's internet traffic through a VPN server, masking their IP address, and encrypting their data.
