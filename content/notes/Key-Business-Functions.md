# Key Business Functions

Key business functions are the specialized activities that a company undertakes to operate effectively and achieve its goals. These functions typically include areas like finance, marketing, operations, human resources, sales, nd research & development, each playing a distinct role in the overall su
ccess of the organization. Understanding how these functions interact and contribute to the business is crucial for analyzing performance and identifying areas for improvement.
