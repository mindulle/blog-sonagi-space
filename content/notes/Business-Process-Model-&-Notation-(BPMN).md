# Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN)

Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN) is a graphical representation of business processes, providing a standardized and easy-to-understand method for visualizing different aspects of a business. By using BPMN, UX designers can analyze and optimize business processes and workflows, which ultimat
ely improves the overall user experience.
