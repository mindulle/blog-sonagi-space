# Google's Gemini

Google Gemini is an advanced AI model by Google DeepMind, designed to integrate natural language processing with multimodal capabilities, enabling it to understand and generate not just text but also images, videos, and other data types. It combines generative AI with reasoning skills, making it eff
ective for complex tasks requiring logical analysis and contextual understanding.
