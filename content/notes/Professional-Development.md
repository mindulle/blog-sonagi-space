# Professional Development

Professional development encompasses activities that enhance an individual's skills, knowledge, and expertise within their field. It involves continuous learning and growth through various avenues such as training courses, certifications, conferences, mentorship, and self-directed study. The goal is
 to stay current with industry trends, improve performance, and advance one's career.
