# Oracle Networks

Oracle networks are groups of independent oracles that work together to provide data to a blockchain. Instead of relying on a single source of information, these networks aggregate data from multiple oracles, enhancing reliability and security. This redundancy helps to mitigate the risk of inaccurat
e or manipulated data being fed into smart contracts, making the overall system more robust.
